What is FSC Mentor Match?

FSC Mentor Match is a website where students at Farmingdale can engage in a peer-to-peer mentorship program that allows them to connect with each other. Create a profile and match with students with similar interests who you can relate to.

Who is this website for?

FSC Mentor Match is for any student who may be looking to be more socially enagaged and active on campus, are looking to see their campus from a student persepctive, and may have questions regarding courses, clubs, or campus life.

Why should you join this community?

This website is for and by FSC students. We want to provide a platform for students to express themselves and connect with others. Our matching system will pair you with another student with similar interests!
